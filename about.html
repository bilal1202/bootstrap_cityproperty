COMPANY PROFILE

City Property is a trusted name in Property dealing. We are a well-known and an established "property marketing network " in the city of Karachi. Professionalism, honesty of purpose, dedication words that describe the work ethics of the largest estate agency and the main reason behind its success. Since its inception in 1999, City Property has grown into the largest network of branches in Karachi and is known for its strength, integrity and innovative approach to all client needs pertaining to real estate transactions.

“We build quality homes & relationships”.

At City Property we take customer satisfaction very seriously and are willing to go to extraordinary lengths to meet our customer’s requirements while minimizing risk factors through intelligent operating procedures. Since its inception in 1999 from a modest premises, City Property has grown at an amazing rate and is known today for its integrity and innovative approach to all matters pertaining to real estate transactions.
